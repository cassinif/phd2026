Lecture 1 (12/01/2025, 2h): A-stability, forward Euler, backward Euler,
trapezoidal method, stiffness, hints about computation of matrix exponential
and phi-functions.

Lecture 2 (14/01/2025, 2h): Introduction to exponential integrators, exponential
Euler method, convergence proof, hints on IMEX methods (backward-forward Euler)

Lecture 3 (19/01/2025, 2h): Implementation of exponential Euler and BF Euler for
nonlinear diffusion equation, space discretization with inner nodes,
diagonalization, bounds on the infinity and two norm for matrix exponential.

Lecture 4 (21/01/2025, 2h): Exponential quadrature rules with 1 point
(derivation and full proof) and their implementation.

Lecture 5 (26/01/2025, 2h): Additional assumptions for Schroedinger operator,
connections with semigroup theory. Introduction to higher order integrators
for semilinear equations.

Lecture 6 (28/01/2025, 2h): Exponential Runge--Kutta methods and their
implementation. Lawson methods.
