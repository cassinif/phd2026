Lecture 1 (12/01/2026, 2h): A-stability, forward Euler, backward Euler,
trapezoidal method, stiffness, hints about computation of matrix exponential
and phi-functions.

Lecture 2 (14/01/2026, 2h): Introduction to exponential integrators,
exponential Euler method, convergence proof,
hints on IMEX methods (backward-forward Euler).

Lecture 3 (19/01/2026, 2h): Implementation of exponential Euler and BF Euler
for nonlinear diffusion equation, space discretization with inner nodes,
diagonalization, bounds on the infinity and two norm for matrix exponential.

Lecture 4 (21/01/2026, 2h): Exponential quadrature rules with 1 point
(derivation and full proof) and their implementation.

Lecture 5 (26/01/2026, 2h): Additional assumptions for Schroedinger operator,
connections with semigroup theory. Introduction to higher order integrators
for semilinear equations.

Lecture 6 (28/01/2026, 2h): Exponential Runge--Kutta methods and their
implementation. Lawson methods.

Lecture 7 (02/02/2026, 2h): Taylor and Pad√© formulas for exp, phi1, phi2
functions, (modified) scaling and squaring, Paterson-Stockmeyer algorithm.

Lecture 8 (04/02/2026, 2h): Backward error analysis for Taylor expansion,
interpolation, action of matrix functions.

Lecture 9 (09/02/2026, 2h): Exponential Rosenbrock--Euler method, derivation
and convergence proof, non-autonomous case, (standard) Rosenbrock methods.

Lecture 10 (11/02/2026, 2h): Augmented matrix and Krylov methods.
